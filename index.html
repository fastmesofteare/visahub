Steps for Our Sponsorship

  • Step 1: Fill out the visa application form.
  • Step 2: Upload your international passport.
  • Step 3: We will submit your documents to the embassy.
  • Step 4: If the embassy approves your visa, you will receive an acceptance letter.
  • Step 5: Proceed to pay your fee.
  • Step 6: Your visa will be forwarded to the embassy in your country. The embassy will then invite you, along with your international passport, for stamping.
